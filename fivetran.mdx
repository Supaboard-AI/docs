Supaboard integrates with **Fivetran** to bring data from 100+ SaaS tools, marketing platforms, and third-party services into your workspace — without writing or managing ETL pipelines.

Fivetran-powered connectors are available on the **Business** and **Enterprise** plans.


# How it works

1. **Select a connector** — choose a Fivetran-powered service from the New Connection grid.
2. **Authenticate** — Supaboard opens Fivetran's OAuth flow; you sign in to the source service to grant read access.
3. **Configure** — select the specific accounts, properties, or datasets to sync.
4. **Sync** — Fivetran begins syncing data on its schedule into a queryable form.
5. **Active** — once the first sync completes, the connection appears as **Active** in Supaboard and is available to agents and widgets.

> **Sync schedule vs real-time** — Fivetran syncs on a schedule (typically every hour to every 24 hours depending on the connector and plan). Data in Supaboard reflects the most recent completed sync, not a live stream. Check the connection's **Info** tab to see the last sync timestamp.
